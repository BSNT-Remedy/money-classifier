Ethics Statement

1. Responsible Use

This machine learning model is an educational prototype. Users must be aware that predictions are not guaranteed to be accurate, and the application should not be used for real-world financial decisions or authentication.

2. Transparency

The system displays both prediction labels and confidence scores to ensure transparency. Users can view their aggregated analytics to better understand how the model behaves over time.

3. Privacy

Uploaded or captured images are used solely for generating model predictions. The application only stores:

  • The predicted label
  • The confidence score
  • The associated user account (if logged in)

No raw images or personal biometric data are stored on the server.

4. Data Usage

Prediction history is used for analytics purposes within the app. This allows users and administrators to understand usage patterns and model performance.

5. Limitations and Bias

  • The model may exhibit bias due to the limited dataset used for training.
  • Environmental factors such as lighting or camera quality may affect predictions.
  • Results should not be interpreted as absolute truth.

6. User Safety

The app avoids making harmful or misleading claims. It is the user's responsibility to ensure they do not rely on the model for sensitive or high-risk applications.

7. Commitments

The developers are committed to:

  • Improving accuracy responsibly
  • Maintaining transparency on how predictions are made
  • Ensuring the model remains an educational tool
  • Preventing misuse or misinterpretation
